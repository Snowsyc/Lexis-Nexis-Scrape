The voters argued that the initiative petition violated Mo. Const. art. III, § 50 and that Mo. Rev. Stat. § 116.050 required the initiative to list sections that would be modified or repealed.


OVERVIEW: The court of appeals held that the trial court did not err in finding that the measure's central purpose was the regulation of gambling and gambling revenues.
