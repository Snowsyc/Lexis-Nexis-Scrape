CASE SUMMARY:


PROCEDURAL POSTURE: Defendant was charged in the Circuit Court of Cook County (Illinois), with possession of a controlled substance with intent to deliver. OVERVIEW: Defendant was stopped for failing to use a turn signal. When he was unable to produce a valid driver's license, he was arrested, and his vehicle was searched incident to arrest.
