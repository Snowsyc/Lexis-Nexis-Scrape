PROCEDURAL POSTURE: Defendant appealed a decision by the circuit court (Illinois), denying defendant's petition for post-conviction relief following defendant's conviction for murder and aggravated criminal sexual assault, for which defendant was sentenced to death.

Defendant filed a petition for post-conviction relief, at which the issue of defendant's fitness to participate in post-conviction proceedings was raised. OUTCOME: The court vacated the denial of defendant's petition for post-conviction relief and remanded for further proceedings, because the circuit court erred in allocating the burden of proof at the hearing to determine defendant's fitness to proceed with post-conviction proceedings to defendant.
