An appeal from the Circuit Court for Bay County. Elijah Smiley, Judge.


CASE SUMMARY:


PROCEDURAL POSTURE: Appellant juvenile was adjudicated delinquent after he pled no contest to lewd and lascivious molestation. OVERVIEW: Appellant argued that the trial court erred by denying his motion to dismiss the petition because a trial was not held within 30 days of the probable cause hearing as required by § 394.916, Fla.
