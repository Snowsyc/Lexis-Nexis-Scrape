OUTCOME: The court granted the motion to dismiss as to Anti-Trust Act and Unfair Trade Practices Act claims, because neither statute specifically bound the state. The court denied the motion as to other counts, as plaintiff sought injunctive relief that did not unreasonably interfere with legitimate government function.
