The Pickens Superior Court (Georgia) denied appellants' motion to dismiss or, in the alternative, for summary judgment. Appellants sought interlocutory review.


OVERVIEW: Appellants argued that the sheriff's department was a state agency and that it and its employees were thus subject to the Georgia Tort Claims Act (GTCA), O.C.G.A. § 50-21-20 et seq.
