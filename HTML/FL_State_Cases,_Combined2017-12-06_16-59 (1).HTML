The trial court, following a hearing, denied appellant's motion for change of custody, and granting the grandparent's petition for custody. The trial court relied on Fla. OUTCOME: Order reversed and remanded. Because the trial court relied on statutory authority which had since been found to be unconstitutional, the court was compelled to reverse the order granting custody to the maternal grandparents, and remanded for further consideration of the issues.
