CASE SUMMARY:

PRIOR HISTORY:  [*1] Clay County District Court File No. 14-CR-13-1141.



DISPOSITION: Reversed and remanded.


CASE SUMMARY:


OVERVIEW: HOLDINGS: [1]-The officer observed defendant driving erratically close to midnight, and he exhibited indicia of intoxication, plus there were alcoholic beverages in the vehicle and defendant failed several field sobriety tests; [2]-On this record, the officer had probable cause to believe that defendant was driving while impaired, and thus police had the option to obtain a test of defendant's blood, breath, or urine by search warrant; [3]-Because that test, either by warrant or defendant's consent, would have been constitutionally reasonable for Fourth Amendment purposes, prosecuting him for test refusal under Minn.
