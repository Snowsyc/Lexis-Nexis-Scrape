PROCEDURAL POSTURE: Appellant county appealed the Circuit Court of Jackson County's (Oregon) grant of summary judgment in favor of respondent public employees on respondents' 42 U.S.C.S. § 1983 and breach of contract claims, and respondents cross-appealed the trial court's grant of summary judgment in favor of appellant on respondents' wage claim.
