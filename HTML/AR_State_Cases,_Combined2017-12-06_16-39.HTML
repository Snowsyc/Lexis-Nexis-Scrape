Counsel was not ineffective for failing to move for dismissal based upon destruction of allegedly exculpatory evidence as the videotape did not contain either admissible or exculpatory information. Defendant had the ability to pursue a coram nobis writ while pursuing postconviction relief under Rule 37.1, but elected not to do so.


OUTCOME: The judgment was affirmed.
