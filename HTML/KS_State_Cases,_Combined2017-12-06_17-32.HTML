OUTCOME: The court affirmed the judgment of the trial court.

PROCEDURAL POSTURE: Appellant escapee challenged the denial of his Kan. Stat. Ann. § 60-1507 motion, which claimed the statute under which he was charged, convicted, and sentenced violated his equal protection rights, by the Reno District Court (Kansas).


OVERVIEW: The escapee challenged the constitutionality of Kan. Stat. Ann. § 21-3810(a)(7) (2001).
