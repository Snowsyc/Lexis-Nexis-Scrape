PRIOR HISTORY:  [***1] Appeal from order granting partial summary judgment. Eighth Judicial District Court, Clark County; Joseph S. Pavlikowski, Judge.



DISPOSITION: Affirmed in part and reversed in part. 


CASE SUMMARY:


PROCEDURAL POSTURE: Appellant county sought review from an order of the Eighth Judicial District Court, Clark County (Nevada), which granted partial summary judgment to respondent city and declared that portions of Nev. Rev. Stat. §§ 280.100 and 280.201 were unconstitutional. The challenged chapter mandated consolidation of county and city police agencies in any city that was the county seat of a county with a population of 200,000 or more.


OVERVIEW: The county argued that the trial court erred when it found Nev. Rev. Stat. §§ 280.100 and 280.201 to be special or local legislation prohibited by the Nev. Const. art. IV, §§ 20, 21, and 25. The trial court declared Nev. Rev. Stat. § 280.100 to be unconstitutional as originally enacted because it included the date of July 1, 1973, which operated as an absolute cutoff beyond which no additional cities or counties would be compelled by law to merge. Only two counties met the requirements on that date. The court held that the trial court erred in holding that the act was special legislation, because an amendment had removed the date, thereby validating the act. Although the trial court correctly found the legislative purpose of the challenged law was to reduce duplication of functions and expenses and to coordinate law enforcement efforts throughout the metropolitan areas, the court held that it erred in finding that the purpose would not be served by the law's population-based mandatory merger provisions. The court held that Nev. Rev. Stat. § 280.201(1)(a) was constitutional because an unconstitutional specification was only added by later amendment and not in the original enactment.


OUTCOME: The trial court affirmed an unchallenged portion of the trial court's grant of partial summary judgment regarding the unconstitutionality of the change of a population figure in the chapter from 200,000 to 250,000. However, the court reversed the balance of the trial court's order granting partial summary judgment to the city.
