CASE SUMMARY:


PROCEDURAL POSTURE: Defendant Village of Long Grove (Long Grove) sought review of an order by the Circuit Court of Lake County (Illinois), which granted plaintiff Village of Buffalo Grove's (Buffalo Grove) motion for summary judgment on Buffalo Grove's complaint in quo warranto, denied Long Grove's motion for summary judgment, and denied Buffalo Grove's motion for sanctions against Long Grove pursuant to Ill. OVERVIEW: Long Grove contended that the Buffalo Grove annexation was invalid and that Buffalo Grove was barred under the doctrine of laches. OUTCOME: The court affirmed the order granting plaintiff's motion for summary judgment, reversed the order denying plaintiff's motion for sanctions, and remanded the cause to the trial court for determination of an appropriate sanction.
