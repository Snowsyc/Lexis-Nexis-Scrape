OVERVIEW: The decedent created an inter vivos trust while he resided in Massachusetts. He died in Maine where he left a substantial estate. OUTCOME: The court reversed the trial court's decree and directed the trial court to enter a decree declaring that the executor was not entitled to apportionment of the federal estate tax as against the trustee.
