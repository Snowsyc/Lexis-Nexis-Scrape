DISPOSITION: AFFIRMED.


CASE SUMMARY:


PROCEDURAL POSTURE: Plaintiff elected official sought review of an adverse judgment against him from the Johnson District Court (Iowa), in his suit to recover attorney's fees from defendant Johnson County Board of Supervisors, which arose from the official's successful defense of a contest to his election to the Board of Supervisors.


OVERVIEW: The official contended the trial court erred when it refused to award him attorney fees in his successful defense to his election to the board of supervisors. The trial court denied relief on the ground that the official was not acting in his official capacity in defending the election challenge. The official argued that Iowa Code § 336.2(6) gave rise to an implied contract between him and the board which required the board to pay his attorneys' fees.
