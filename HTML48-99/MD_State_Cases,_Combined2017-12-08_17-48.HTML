PROCEDURAL POSTURE: Plaintiff property owner (owner) challenged an order of the Circuit Court for Frederick County (Maryland), which affirmed defendant Planning Commission's decision that denied the owner's application for a site plan.

Indeed, the Planning Commission was required to approve the application unless it was clear that it did not meet the objective requirements outlined in the zoning ordinance. As such, the matter was remanded to the Planning Commission.


OUTCOME: The court reversed the trial court's order, which affirmed the Planning Commission's decision to deny the owner's application for a site plan, and remanded to the Planning Commission for further proceedings.
