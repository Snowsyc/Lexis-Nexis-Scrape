PROCEDURAL POSTURE: Appellant corporate taxpayer sought review of a judgment of the District Court of Lewis and Clark County (Montana) that affirmed a decision by the State Tax Appeal Board (Board), which upheld a decision of the Department of Revenue that disallowed a net operating loss deduction from taxable income claimed by the taxpayer, pursuant to Mont. OVERVIEW: Two corporations entered into a merger. The surviving corporation's deduction for net operating losses sustained by the two corporations was disallowed. The Board affirmed the decision. The statute prevented all merged corporations from deducting pre-merger net operating losses.


OUTCOME: The court affirmed the judgment of the trial court.
