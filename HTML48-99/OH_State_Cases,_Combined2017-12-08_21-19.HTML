CASE SUMMARY:


PROCEDURAL POSTURE: Relator state auditor brought an action for writ of prohibition to prevent respondent judge of the common pleas court from hearing plaintiff dentist's injunction action against the auditor, defendant State, and defendant state agency.


OVERVIEW: The dentist brought an injunction action against the State, the auditor, and the state agency to compel payment for dental services provided to welfare patients pursuant to a contract with the State. The auditor brought an action for a writ of prohibition to prevent the common pleas court from hearing the case. The court issued a limited writ that solely prohibited the action against the State.
