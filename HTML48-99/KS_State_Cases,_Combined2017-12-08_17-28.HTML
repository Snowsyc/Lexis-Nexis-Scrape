PROCEDURAL POSTURE: Appellant property owner sought review of a judgment from the Geary District Court (Kansas), which ruled in favor of appellee neighboring residents in their cause of action to obtain a permanent injunction against the owner's construction of a 40-foot by 60-foot detached garage on her property.


OVERVIEW: The property owner appealed the trial court's grant of a permanent injunction against construction of a 40-foot by 60-foot detached garage on her property.
