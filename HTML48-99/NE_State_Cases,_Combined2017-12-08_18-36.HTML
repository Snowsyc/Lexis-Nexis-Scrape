OVERVIEW: The question the court had to determine was whether the classifications embraced within the Sunday Closing law had some reasonable relation to the promotion of health, peace, and the good order of society, and were based upon reasonable distinctions affecting all persons similarly situated or engaged in the same type of business without discrimination.
