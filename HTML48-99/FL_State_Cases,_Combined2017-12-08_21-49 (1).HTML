CASE SUMMARY:


PROCEDURAL POSTURE: Appellant sought review of the order from the Circuit Court of Clay County (Florida) that assessed points for nine prior convictions, departed upward from the presumptive sentence of the sentencing guidelines, and imposed costs under Fla. OVERVIEW: Appellant pled guilty to two counts of uttering a forged instrument. The court held that the trial court erred by assessing points for appellant's prior misdemeanor convictions, departing upward for reasons that were used to arrive at appellant's presumptive sentence, and failing to determine appellant's ability to pay before assessing coats.
