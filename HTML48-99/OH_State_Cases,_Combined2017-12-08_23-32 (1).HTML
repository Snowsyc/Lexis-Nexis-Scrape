PROCEDURAL POSTURE: Plaintiff local church appealed from the judgment of the Seneca County Common Pleas Court (Ohio) which dismissed its complaint to declare void a deed conveying a church building and land from defendant predecessor to defendant parent church and quieted title to the subject property in the parent church, which had not sought any affirmative relief.


OVERVIEW: The subject property was conveyed to the trustees of a certain church that later merged with the parent church.
